Brian P. Griffiths

In the middle of 2019 I was living in Wuhan China, yes the city the Coronavirus came from. I was lecturing American History and had been living there the last four years. I decided I had, had enough of China and returned to the US just a few months before the outbreak. Now stuck in doors and not wanting to waste any time, I applied to Lambda. I have always been fascinated by technology, I was born in the 90s and have witnessed incredible technological progress. The fear of regret is what drives me, I am afraid I will blink and be an old man, and I will lament how I should have gotten more involved in the digital age.
